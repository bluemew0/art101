Introduction

Hi! I'm Ashley and I'm a 4th year game art and design major (AGPM) at UCSC! I'm interested in creating art for games and as freelance, but I've also recently developed an interest in web design after taking a few classes that practice JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. As such, I hope to use this site as a way to track my progress and projects! This website was originally made for my ART 101 class which was programming for the arts.

My Interests

  • Digital Art

    While I grew up with traditional art and have a strong background in it, over the years, I have grown to love digital art and it has become my main medium when drawing. I enjoy making anime-style artwork and fanart.

  • Arts & Crafts

    I have a lot of fun making small crafts or artistic pieces as gifts for friends and family. I've done cross-stitching, worked with shrink plastic, making postcards, all sorts of small things. In my childhood, I also had a polymer clay phase but that didn't last too long.

  • Playing Games

    Lately, I've been playing a lot of Genshin Impact, but I also enjoy the Pokemon and Zelda series. I'm also a big fan of Hades and Animal Crossing: New Horizons.
